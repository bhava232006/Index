big data

Big data is a collection of large, complex, and diverse data sets that are difficult to manage using traditional data management tools. It can include structured, unstructured, and semi-structured data. How does big data grow? The amount of data available is growing rapidly due to digital technology advancements like the Internet of Things (IoT) and artificial intelligence (AI). The cost of storage and compute has decreased significantly, making it easier and less expensive to store more data. How is big data used? Organizations use big data to drive decisions, improve processes and policies, and create customer-centric products and services. Big data is used in machine learning, predictive modeling, and other advanced analytics applications. How do we analyze big data? Big data analytics can uncover previously hidden trends, patterns, and correlations. This information can help leaders and decision-makers make better business decisions. What are the characteristics of big data? Big data is big in volume, velocity, variety, and veracity. It can come from anywhere or anything on earth that we're able to monitor digitally.
